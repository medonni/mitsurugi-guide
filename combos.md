# Main combo line 1

## Step 0 - Get to Habakiri
- Pre-prep
- Mitsurugi Prayers
- Aramasa normal summon
- Ragged ritual of rites

### Next steps might vary a bit, depending on other cards in hand and how do you get to Habakiri in step 0

## Step 1 - Habakiri activate effect in hand
- Reveal Habakiri
- Special summon Saji
- Tribute Saji
- Watch out for Ash Blossoms

## Step 1.5 - Saji triggers in GY
- Add Mitsurugi Ritual
- Watch out for Droll after

## Step 2 - Activate ritual to Ritual summon from hand, tribute from deck
- Ritual summon Habakiri
- Tribute Aramasa and Kusanagi
- Can use NSS instead of Aramasa to revive it, if running it.

## Step 2.5 - Tributed monsters
- Tributed monsters trigger in grave 
- CL1 Kusanagi to return mirror
- CL2 Aramasa to fetch Futsu

## Step 3 - Activate ritual to Ritual summon from Deck, tribute from field
- Tribute Habakiri to summon Murakumo from deck

## Step 3.5 - Habakiri activation
- Habakiri on tribute effect activates, adding a mitsurugi card and reviving itself
- Add Mitsurugi Mirror and revive Habakiri

## Step 4 - Mirror activation
- Activate Mitsurugi Mirror, tributing Murakumo to summon Futsu from hand in defense position
- Futsu is 3400 def, really big defender

## Step 4.5 - Murakumo activation
- Murakumo now activates in grave
- Adds a mitsurugi card and revives itself
- Can add either Trap or Mitsurugi Mirror
